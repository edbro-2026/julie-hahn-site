Biography

A Life
Devoted to Music

Classical concert pianist and professor of music Julie Juyun Hahn has appeared as a solo pianist, accompanist, chamber musician, and conductor. She has given numerous piano solo recitals in various locations such as in Houston, Austin, Madison, Baltimore, Washington, D.C in the U.S.A; Perugia and Assisi in Italy; Seoul and Busan in Korea.

Julie performed as a prize winner with the Peabody Chamber Orchestra, the Camerata Ensemble, the University of Texas Orchestra, as well as the Busan Philharmonic Orchestra. Julie has performed with the chamber orchestra I Solisti di Perugia while participating in an international piano festival (Music Fest Perugia) in 2007 in Italy.

She studied at the Peabody Conservatory (Johns Hopkins), the Catholic University of America, and the University of Texas at Austin. Julie is a National Certified Teacher of Music (NCTM) and has been on the faculty at Northern Virginia Community College since 2005.

Studio Policy

Julie Hahn
Piano Studio

Tuition & Fees

Tuition is charged monthly, payable in advance at the first lesson of the month. The monthly payment is calculated as: (Lesson Fee) × (Number of weekly lessons in the month). The tuition rate varies by student level or age and is determined at an initial interview.

Missed Lessons & Lateness

Unexcused absences cannot be made up. A make-up lesson will be scheduled only with 24-hour advance notice, up to two times per semester. Lessons canceled by the teacher will always be rescheduled. Any time missed due to lateness will be forfeited unless otherwise arranged.

Inclement Weather & Holidays

The studio closes when schools are closed due to severe weather, and lessons will be rescheduled. The studio is closed on Memorial Day, Thanksgiving, summer vacation (late June–late August), and winter break (approx. Dec. 22–Jan. 3).

Practice & Lesson Termination

Daily practice is essential for satisfactory progress. A 30-day written notice is required to terminate lessons at the end of a semester. No refund is given for unused lessons within the final month.
